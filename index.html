The machine

I don't just talk about AI in game production. I've built it into my daily workflow across two live production environments and my own game development pipeline.

The principle is always the same: AI handles the information layer, humans make the decisions. That's where genuine ROI comes from — not chasing tools, but redesigning workflows around what AI is actually good at.

I can distinguish AI hype from genuine utility, and I know how hard it is to roll this out inside a real company with real teams and real resistance. That's the problem I find genuinely interesting.
